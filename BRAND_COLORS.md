# StayAfrica Website Brand & Color Guide

This guide outlines how to apply the StayAfrica color palette to the web application UI. The goal is to create a "Luxury Safari" aesthetic that is sophisticated, earthy, and user-friendly.

## 1. Header & Navigation Bar
* **Background:** `Deep Forest (#122F26)`
    * *Effect:* Grounds the top of the site and maximizes logo visibility.
* **Navigation Links:** `Ivory Sand (#F4F1EA)`
    * *Hover State:* Change text color to `Safari Gold (#D9B168)`.
* **"Sign Up" / "Host" Button:** Ghost/Outline Style.
    * *Border & Text:* `Safari Gold (#D9B168)`
    * *Background:* Transparent.

## 2. Hero Section (Top Banner)
* **Background Image:** High-quality accommodation or landscape photo.
* **Overlay:** `Deep Forest (#122F26)` with **40-60% opacity**.
    * *Reason:* Ensures white text is legible over complex images.
* **Headline Text:** `Ivory Sand (#F4F1EA)` or `Pure White (#FFFFFF)`.
* **Primary CTA Button:** (e.g., "Find a Stay")
    * *Background:* `Safari Gold (#D9B168)`
    * *Text:* `Deep Forest (#122F26)`
    * *Note:* Dark text on gold provides better accessibility than white text on gold.

## 3. Property Listing Cards
* **Page Background:** `Ivory Sand (#F4F1EA)` (Do not use pure white for the whole page).
* **Card Surface:** `Pure White (#FFFFFF)` with a subtle shadow.
* **Property Title:** `Deep Forest (#122F26)`.
* **Price:** `Deep Forest (#122F26)` (Bold weight).
* **Rating Icons (Stars):** `Safari Gold (#D9B168)`.
* **"Book Now" / Action Button:** `Deep Forest (#122F26)` background with `White` text.

## 4. Typography & Text Hierarchy
* **Headings (H1 - H3):** `Deep Forest (#122F26)`
    * *Style:* Serif font (e.g., Playfair Display).
* **Body Text:** `Savanna Text (#0A1A15)`
    * *Style:* Sans-serif (e.g., Inter or Lato).
    * *Note:* Avoid pure black (`#000000`); this dark green-black is softer on the eyes.
* **Meta Data (Dates, Guests, Subtitles):** `Moss Green (#3A5C50)`.

## 5. Footer
* **Background:** `Deep Forest (#122F26)`.
* **Text Links:** `Ivory Sand (#F4F1EA)`.
* **Social Icons:** `Safari Gold (#D9B168)`.
* **Dividers/Borders:** `Moss Green (#3A5C50)` (Low opacity).

### 1. The "Gold" Trap
* ‚ùå **DON'T** use Gold text on a White background. It creates poor contrast and is hard to read.
* ‚úÖ **DO** use Gold for icons, buttons, borders, and backgrounds on dark sections.

### 2. Whitespace is Luxury
* Crowded interfaces look cheap. Use generous padding around cards and text.
* Let the `Ivory Sand` background show through to establish the earthy brand feel.

### 3. Visual Hierarchy
To guide the user's eye, use color to denote importance:
1.  **High Priority (Search/Pay):** Gold Background.
2.  **Medium Priority (Details/Info):** Dark Green Background.
3.  **Low Priority (Cancel/Back):** Underlined Text or Ghost Button.
